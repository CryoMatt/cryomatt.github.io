Database values are loaded automatically from the selected country. Client override fields are optional.

Formulas: conventional = L/day × 365 × LN₂ price or CO₂. Fusion = kWh/year × electricity price + LN₂ usage. Capacity = (standard racks × boxes/rack × 100 vials) + (small racks × boxes/rack × 25 vials). Savings are calculated per vial and then multiplied by Fusion total vials. Cost per vial is displayed with extra decimals because values are small.
